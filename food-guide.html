Within the Fuzhou Province, Peanut Butter noodles were created as a quick and easy meal, especially for those who just came back from a hard-working day. With just some scallions, wheat noodles, and of course, peanut butter, you can make this simply in your own little abode!
Peanut Butter Noodles

If you're into spicy food, you've gotta try Mapo Tofu! It's a famous dish from Sichuan Province in China that's perfect for adventurous eaters. Imagine tender tofu cubes cooked in a fiery chili sauce with minced meat—it's flavorful and packs a punch! One great place to try it is at Spicy Panda Restaurant, located at 123 Sichuan Street, Chengdu, Sichuan, China. They serve up the best Mapo Tofu in town, along with other mouthwatering Sichuan dishes. Whether you're a spice aficionado or just looking to try something new, Mapo Tofu at Spicy Panda is a must-try!
Mapo Tofu

If you’re in Beijing and craving something special, head over to Quack Quack Duck House for some amazing Peking Duck. Located at 456 Duck Street, Beijing, China, this place is famous for its crispy duck skin and tender meat, served with thin pancakes, hoisin sauce, and fresh veggies. It’s perfect for travelers who love trying local delicacies and want to experience authentic Chinese cuisine. Don’t miss out on this delicious treat—it’s a must-try while you’re exploring Beijing!
